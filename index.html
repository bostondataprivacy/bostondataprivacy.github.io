Boston-Area Data Privacy

Welcome to the web page for the Boston-area data privacy research group! We are faculty, postdocs, students, and research staff at Boston University, Harvard and Northeastern working on the foundations of data privacy and related topics.

We're excited to host the next installment of Privacy Day on April 11, 2025! See Privacy Day!
